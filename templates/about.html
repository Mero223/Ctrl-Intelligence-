    Who we are

    Made by students, ReVidalize is a website aiming to help people disconnect from their phone and reconnect with themselves. Also as victims of screen fatigue from working from home, we wanted to find a way to encourage getting away from the screen. That is how ReVidalize was created.

    Though there are many applications out there that will press users to limit scren time, there is a gap in telling what users to do during that off-screen time. ReVidalize is an app that not only limits screen time, but also helps users find activities to do to reconnect with themselves.

    Why ReVidalize?

    What is ReVidalize?

    ReVidalize is a website that gives you options to reconnect with yourself through different outlets. Maybe you'd like to clear your mind through meditation, learn a new recipe, read some short stories, stimulate your creativity through our creative prompts, or enjoy nature outside. Don't know what you want to do? ReVidalize will give you a random activity to do if you choose the "random" option. The possibilites are endless for you to reconnect with yourself.
